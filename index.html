Services

These are the list of things I can provide.

Loving

My specialty is giving love to humans. I have three humans that takes care of me and loves to cuddle with me. They say, When I look into their eyes, they feel love. If you wish to feel the love, I'm on it!

Warmth

My body temperature is higher than humans. When they hug me they feel warmth. Back in the days, I was used to make humans warm since thermostat wasn't invented. I can provide warmth to humans whenever, wherever!

House Protector

If you are scared to live alone, I can be your house bodyguard. If new human visits or knocks on a door, I'll bark as loud as I can to scare them. You won't feel scared if I'm with you.

About

  • 2013-2014

    Beginning of My Life

    I was born in a house with 5 siblings. 4 of them got adopted by a beautiful family. Although seperating with my family was hard, I was able to move on beacause of my three humans that gave me much love.

  • 2014-2018

    Living with Granny

    My humans had to go overseas for their academical studies and I wasn't able to go with them. They promised me that they'll come back. It was sad but I loved granny so much. she gave so many treats that I gained 4kg.

  • 2018-

    Current life

    My humans came back as they promised and our family is back together! Though they don't give treats like granny, I love them.
